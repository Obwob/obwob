# Obwob

**Catch feedback before it slips away. Build evidence as you go.**

Obwob is a research-led platform for live feedback and impact evidence, designed for settings where attention, reflection and meaningful responses matter.

---

## What Obwob is

Obwob enables facilitators to pose questions in real time and gather responses through a simple scan-and-respond flow. Participants join via QR code on their own devices — no downloads, no logins, no barriers.

The platform supports both facilitator-led moments (questions pushed live during sessions) and participant-led reflection (spontaneous responses when insight emerges). Optional participant accounts enable longitudinal tracking across sessions and over time — capturing evidence of change as it unfolds, not reconstructed afterwards.

## What makes it different

Obwob sits between real-time engagement tools (Mentimeter, Slido) and traditional survey platforms (Qualtrics, SurveyMonkey).

Unlike presentation tools, Obwob offers data persistence, longitudinal tracking, and evidence-focused outputs. Unlike post-event questionnaires, Obwob is designed for live, facilitated moments — feedback built into the work, not bolted on afterwards.

## Research foundations

Obwob emerged from [Objects Without Borders](https://objectswithoutborders.com), a practice-based research project exploring how object performance creates connection across distance, difference and disruption. Working with arts organisations, displaced communities and participatory settings revealed the limits of existing digital tools — and the need for something designed from the ground up for reflective engagement.

The platform draws on over two decades of research in puppetry, performance and participatory methodologies. Where conventional tools treat feedback as something collected afterwards, Obwob embeds reflective moments directly within live activities — capturing insight while it still matters.

This work is grounded in pedagogically informed methods for impact, where structured reflection during an activity deepens learning and engagement while also producing robust evidence.

## Who it's for

Obwob is used across charities, universities, healthcare, HR and consultancy — anywhere facilitators need to collect meaningful feedback, guide participation and capture insights as part of a live activity, not as an afterthought.

## How it's built

Obwob is built with HTMX, React, Django and Ably for real-time messaging, deployed on Render PaaS. The architecture is designed for live use: lightweight, fast and responsive, with strong separation of concerns. GDPR-compliant, human-authored and human-controlled.

## Current status

Obwob is in active development, with pilot testing underway across multiple sectors. The platform is supported by UKRI funding through ESRC/AHRC SHAPE Catalyst and Impact Acceleration Account programmes.

Pilot places are available from May 2026.
